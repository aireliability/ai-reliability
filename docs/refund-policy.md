# AI Reliability Refund Policy

AI Reliability subscriptions are generally **non-refundable once access is granted**.

If a duplicate payment occurs, contact billing support at `billing@aireliability.com` to resolve the duplicate charge (activated after domain, company, and payment-provider readiness).

If payment is taken but access is not delivered, contact support at `support@aireliability.com` so we can investigate and resolve the issue (activated after domain, company, and payment-provider readiness).
