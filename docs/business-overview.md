# AI Reliability — Business Overview

## What the product is
AI Reliability is a developer infrastructure product that helps teams catch bad AI deploys before they ship.

## What problem it solves
It prevents faulty AI outputs, policy mistakes, missed required actions, and other high-risk failures from reaching production.

## Who it is for
It is for solo technical founders, product teams, and software businesses shipping AI into real production workflows.

## How it works
Teams run evals locally and in CI before deployment. Failed evals return non-zero exit codes so bad AI changes can be blocked before release.

## Commercial model
AI Reliability is sold as a subscription product with usage-based credits.

Starter — $299/month — 1,000 credits
Team — $999/month — 5,000 credits
Growth — $2,500/month — 15,000 credits

1 credit = 1 sample evaluation.

## Why it matters
A single bad AI deploy can create support load, refund mistakes, hallucinated business information, or silent operational failures. AI Reliability is designed to stop those failures before production.
